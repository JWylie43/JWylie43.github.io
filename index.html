I have a passion and love for software development. I studied Electrical Engineering at Texas State Universirty from 2017 to 2021. Durring my final semester, while working for a startup, I started gain a love for software development. It was while working for this company that I realized that the repetetiveness of the task I was performing could probably be automated by use of software.
The only programming language I had used to any serious degree at the time, thanks to my capstone project for school, was Python. It was right then that I started researching and learning some of the many things that a language like python could allow me to do. My determination, desire to prove to myself that I could do it and my eagerness to come up with a program that would allow me to continue working full time while taking a full class load was what drove me to become intrenched in the world of software development. I quickly developed a program that helped me do my job at work faster and with less involvment so that I could free up some time for me to complete school work while my job was being completed by the computer.
It was this feeling of accomplishment and satisfaction combined with my desire to work smarter and not harder that propelled my interest in software development. I have become obsessed with the idea of automating, simplfiying and even sometimes overcomplicating everything just so long as I can learn a new skill to add into my developers tool kit. I will continue to expaned on my knowledge and skillset with real work applications as well as personal prjects.
